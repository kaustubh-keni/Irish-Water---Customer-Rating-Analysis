Name: Kaustubh Keni¶

Introduction¶

Welcome to the analysis of the Irish Water Customer Survey Data. This project aims to preprocess and analyze the survey responses to uncover valuable insights about customer satisfaction and service quality.

Objectives:¶

  • Data Preprocessing: Cleaning and preparing the data for analysis.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Understanding the data through summary statistics and visualizations.
  • Insights Extraction: Identifying key patterns and insights from the data.

Dataset:¶

The dataset consists of responses from customers of Irish Water. Each row represents a unique respondent, and the columns contain various survey questions and demographic information.

Tools:¶

  • Python: The primary programming language used for this analysis.
  • Pandas: For data manipulation and analysis.
  • NumPy: For numerical operations.
  • Matplotlib/Seaborn: For data visualization.
  • Jupyter Notebook: For documenting the analysis process interactively.

Let's dive into the data preprocessing and analysis to derive meaningful insights from the customer survey responses.
